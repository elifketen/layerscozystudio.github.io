About Layers Cozy

At Layers Cozy Studio, we believe every brand deserves a visual identity that feels layered, intentional, and welcoming. Founded in 2024, our small team specializes in crafting logos, color systems, and complete brand toolkits for emerging businesses, boutiques, and creative projects.
